About Us

Welcome to Zircon Overseas saddlery company a unit of Zircon Overseas founded by Mr. S.H. Hifzan, the owner and CEO of the company. We take pride in being a manufacturer, designer, and customizer of horse riding and pet saddlery products. At Zircon Overseas saddlery company we are dedicated to providing high-quality leather saddlery and pet items. We specialize in manufacturing and exporting all kinds of saddlery products, including saddles, bridles, halters, rugs, saddle pads, and other accessories. We use only the finest materials and employ skilled craftsmen to ensure that our products are of the highest quality. Mr. S.H Hifzan, the founder of Zircon Overseas saddlery company has been in this business from an early age. He learned the basics of manufacturing horse riding accessories, pets and other saddlery products like saddles, bridles, halters and rugs from his family members who had over 40 years of experience in this business of manufacturing and exporting. In 2018, Mr. S.H Hifzan and his team started their own company named Zircon Overseas saddlery company with the aim of providing premium quality saddlery and pet products to customers. The company is backed by the expertise of the Zircon Overseas factory, which specializes in making top-notch quality saddlery and pet products. Providing our best quality products according to buyers' requirements, we also supply to several American, European, and Middle Eastern companies and manufacture their products in our factory with their own private labels. Whether you are a horse, dog or cat product seller, we have a wide range of products to meet your needs. Thank you for choosing Zircon Overseas saddlery company as your go-to destination for high-quality saddlery and pet products.

ABOUT ZIRCON OVERSEAS SADDLERY

My team and I are committed to making this sector even better by developing new and innovative premium quality equestrian and pet leather products.
